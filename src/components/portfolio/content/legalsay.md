## Overview

LegalSay is an AI-powered legal assistant designed to streamline legal research, document analysis, and case preparation. The platform uses advanced natural language processing to understand complex legal queries and provide accurate, citation-backed responses.

Legal professionals use LegalSay to reduce research time by up to 70%, allowing them to focus on strategic legal work rather than manual document review and case law research.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Material-UI
- **Backend**: Python (FastAPI), PostgreSQL, Redis
- **AI/ML**: OpenAI GPT-4, Custom Legal NLP Models, Vector Embeddings
- **Search**: Elasticsearch, Pinecone (Vector Database)
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda), Docker
- **Authentication**: JWT, OAuth 2.0

## Key Features

- **Legal Research**: Natural language queries for case law, statutes, and regulations
- **Document Analysis**: AI-powered contract review and risk assessment
- **Citation Verification**: Automatic citation checking and validation
- **Case Summarization**: Generate concise summaries of lengthy legal documents
- **Precedent Finder**: Identify relevant case precedents based on fact patterns
- **Secure Document Storage**: End-to-end encrypted document repository
- **Collaboration Tools**: Share research and annotations with team members
